About Us

KiosinAja is a platform that helps kiosk providers market their kiosks to entrepreneurs seeking business space. We aim to simplify the process of renting, buying, or selling kiosks in Indonesia.

Features

  • Easy kiosk listing for providers
  • Search and filter kiosks by location and size
  • Secure transactions with verified users
  • Dedicated support for renters and providers
